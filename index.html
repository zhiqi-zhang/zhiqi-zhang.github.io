Biography

I am currently a Master student major in Psychology at Beijing Normal University, China. The GPA is 3.8/4.0. Previously, I received the Bachelor of psychology degree from School of Psychology in Beijing Normal University, 2018.

My research interests include self-other cognition, intergroup relationship and moral emotion (such as pride, guilt and gratitude). I am dedicated to combine traditional experimental psychology and cognitive neuroscience method (fMRI, tDCS, fNIRS) to investigate problems on social cognition.
